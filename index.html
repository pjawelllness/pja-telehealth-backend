üíª Telehealth Informed Consent

What is Telehealth?

Telehealth uses secure video conferencing to provide healthcare services remotely.

Benefits

  • Access care from the comfort of your home
  • Reduced travel time and costs
  • Convenient scheduling
  • Continuity of care

Limitations & Risks

  • Technical Issues: Internet connectivity may affect call quality
  • Clinical Limitations: Physical examination is limited compared to in-person visits
  • Emergencies: Telehealth is NOT appropriate for medical emergencies. Call 911 for emergencies.

Your Responsibilities

  • Ensure reliable internet connection and compatible device
  • Participate from a private, quiet location
  • Be prepared to verify your identity
  • Understand that in-person care may be needed for certain conditions

Platform Security

We use Doxy.me, a HIPAA-compliant video platform, for all consultations. No software download is required.

Payment & Fees

Payment is required at time of booking. We accept HSA/FSA cards and major credit cards. Services are self-pay only.
