🔒 HIPAA Privacy Notice

Effective Date: October 19, 2025

How We Use Your Health Information

Your health information may be used and disclosed for:

  • Treatment: Providing, coordinating, or managing your healthcare
  • Payment: Processing payments for services provided
  • Healthcare Operations: Quality improvement and training

Your Rights

  • Access and review your medical records
  • Request amendments to your records
  • Request restrictions on how we use your information
  • Receive confidential communications
  • File a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

Our Responsibilities

  • Maintain the privacy and security of your health information
  • Provide you with this notice of our privacy practices
  • Follow the terms of this notice
  • Notify you of any breaches of your protected health information

Secure Platform

All health information is stored in Square's HIPAA-compliant system. Video consultations are conducted through Doxy.me, a HIPAA-compliant telemedicine platform with a signed Business Associate Agreement.

💻 Telehealth Informed Consent

What is Telehealth?

Telehealth uses secure video conferencing to provide healthcare services remotely.

Benefits

  • Access care from the comfort of your home
  • Reduced travel time and costs
  • Convenient scheduling
  • Continuity of care

Limitations & Risks

  • Technical Issues: Internet connectivity may affect call quality
  • Clinical Limitations: Physical examination is limited compared to in-person visits
  • Emergencies: Telehealth is NOT appropriate for medical emergencies. Call 911 for emergencies.

Your Responsibilities

  • Ensure reliable internet connection and compatible device
  • Participate from a private, quiet location
  • Be prepared to verify your identity
  • Understand that in-person care may be needed for certain conditions

Platform Security

We use Doxy.me, a HIPAA-compliant video platform, for all consultations. No software download is required.

Payment & Fees

Payment is required at time of booking. We accept HSA/FSA cards and major credit cards. Services are self-pay only.

📹 Session Recording Policy

Your telehealth session may be recorded for quality assurance, training, and medical record purposes. Recordings are stored securely and treated as part of your medical record.
